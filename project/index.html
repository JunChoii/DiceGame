• You will play against the computer.
  • Both players will roll two dice, 3 times.
  • The player with the highest total after 3 rounds wins the match.
  • When both players roll the same total, it is a tie.
  • When you get a 1 on any of your dice, you score 0 for that round.
  • When the pair of the dice is the same, the score will be doubled.
  • Otherwise, the score will be the sum of the two dice.
  • Roll button will roll the dices and every round, you roll them
  • Reset button will reset all the score and you can play new game
